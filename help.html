Help

You're not the only one in search
  • How do I find the best result for my search?

    The preferences chosen affect the result. The first preference choice will have a greater weight over other preferences.

  • How updated is the information?

    We use public information provided by Hong Kong government and local property agencies. We will update the information on daily basis.

  • Can I use the app without an account?

    Yes. You can use the search feature to look for apartments. However, once you want to use other functions like saving the search results into your favorite list, you must login to let the app identify you.

  • How do reviews work?

    Basically, the reviews are all from our users. They are all user generated contents. You may use them as your references but we hold no responsibility to all the review contents.

  • How can I manage my account?

    You can only manage your own account inside the app. We do not provide account management function in our website.

  • How do I know the meaning of the error messages?

    When you see any error message or encounter any problem using the app, we suggest you to contact us. Our customer service representitives will be there to help you.
