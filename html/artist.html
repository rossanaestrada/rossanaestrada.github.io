Dance and Arts

The meaning to me


Dance is an universal language and it is the language of the body in motion listening and interpreting the music, this one being the fuel for the spirit that nourishes our imagination and our passion for life. As it is a form of expression, I believe every single person, no matter how long time is needed, has the natural ability to practice it until domination so then being able to enjoy it and it can be even more enjoyable when the body is well conditioned and prepared so the technique and the expression of emotions reach a level of balanced concordance and harmony. Just needs lots of determination and perseverance. Everything is possible and that's a fact! So don't ever give up if you really want it and always keep motivation in your daily meals! If one perseveres, even a small seed will sprout, grow and eventually blossom beautifully! "Arts, music and dance are the bridge to touch people's hearts and souls". I know I need to improve and to learn so much yet, I know there's no limit and not perfection either, but I know as well how dance changes people's lives to better and how it gives you such a great sense of freedom so that's why I like spreading that joy by helping people to learn this new skill and to experience this new lifestyle full of delight and pleasure.

Quick facts: My speciality is Latin Dance (Salsa, Merengue, Cha Cha, Bachata, Zumba and Lambada). I have been dancing since 6 years old. Currently leading a group of 13 people, teaching my own choreography, organising an event to showcase the group with a performance (22 Aug, 2015)
