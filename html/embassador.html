Culture and Colombia

The meaning to me


Culture is our very first root and it determines who we are and what we should do. It gives us the main basic concepts about our essence, our life and the world around us. I love my culture, my beautiful and colorful Colombia and I want to share with you why. Being passionate about culture is being passionate about many different things: arts, forms of expression, abstract concepts, creative imagination and people! That's a whole world of things to explore and experience! As the old saying says: sometimes you don't know what you've got until, at least momentarily, you don't have it. That chance of loosing it for a little bit enables you to appreciate it more! So give it a shot! Keep being open minded, go out of your comfort zone and explore! Keeping on being 100% Colombian, I have had the amazing opportunity to live in China for many years already and part of its culture it's already within me. At the same time being so passionate about cultures, I've managed to travel to many amazing countries trying to learn and keep the best of them with me. A global village where diverse cultures, visions and beliefs synchronize with each other and coincide with the main purpose of our existence = Happiness. That's my ideal world, what's yours?
