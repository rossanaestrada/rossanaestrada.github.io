Culture and Colombia

The meaning to me


Culture is our very first root and it determines who we are and what we should do. It gives us the main basic concepts about our essence, our life and the world around us. I love my culture, my beautiful and colorful Colombia and I want to share with you why ( Stay tune for interesting and amazing posts about it) ( link goes to a blog entrance just about Colombia for knowledge and interesting facts)

Being passionate about culture is being passionate about many different things: arts, forms of expression, abstract concepts, creative imagination and people! That's a whole world of things to explore and experience !

As the old saying says: sometimes you don't know what you've got until, at least momentarily, you don't have it. That chance of loosing it for a little bit enables you to appreciate it more! So give it a shot! Keep open minded, go out of your comfort place and explore!

Keeping on being 100% Colombian, I have had the amazing opportunity to live in China for many years already and part of its culture it's already within me. ( Stay tuned for my future performances on Asian dances). At the same time being so passionate about cultures, I've managed to travel to many amazing countries trying to learn and keep the best of them with me. I wanna share all this with you - check it out. (That link goes to a traveling blog)

A global village where diverse cultures, visions and beliefs synchronize with each other and coincide with the main purpose of our existence = Happiness.(Link about happiness, other blog entrance idea :p ) That's my ideal world, what's yours? ( link where people can comment and write ideas )
